Biography

I am currently a Senior Algorithm Engineer in the ERNIE (文心) Team at Baidu NLP. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) in 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Tingwen Liu and Prof. Li Guo.

My research interests broadly include Natural Language Processing, Knowledge Discovery, and Multimodal Models. Now, I am also committed to the frontier exploration and practical application of Large Language Models (LLM) and Artificial Intelligence Generated Content (AIGC).

I have published several papers in top-tier conferences such as AAAI, ACL, CVPR, MM, WWW, and won the Best Paper Award of PAKDD 2020.
